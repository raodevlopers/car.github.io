Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our car rental services. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name for the security deposit, and proof of identity (passport or national ID). International renters may need an International Driving Permit along with their native license.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is 21 years. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Some premium or luxury vehicles may have higher minimum age requirements.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period subject to vehicle availability. Please contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled return time to arrange an extension. Additional charges will apply for the extended period.

What happens if I return the car late?

Late returns may be subject to additional charges. We allow a 29-minute grace period. Returns more than 30 minutes late will be charged an extra half-day rate. Returns more than 3 hours late will be charged a full day's rental.

Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

Most of our rentals come with unlimited mileage within the state. For interstate travel, some restrictions may apply. Please check with our staff when booking if you plan to travel long distances.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and cash payments. A security deposit is required for all rentals, which can only be held on a credit card.
